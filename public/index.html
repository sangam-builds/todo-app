Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about TaskFlow? Explore quick answers below or contact our help desk.

How does Google Calendar integration function?

When you authenticate your Google account in the dashboard, TaskFlow links directly to your calendar. Adding a task automatically creates a corresponding time block event on your calendar. Marking a task completed automatically marks the calendar event completed or removes it depending on your preference.

Can I use TaskFlow without creating an account?

Absolutely! You can create and manage tasks anonymously. Your data will be saved securely to your browser's local storage. However, you will need to sign in with Google to synchronize tasks across multiple devices or sync with your calendar.

Does TaskFlow support dark mode?

Yes, TaskFlow features a highly-optimized dark mode enabled by default to reduce eye fatigue. You can toggle between dark and light themes at any time from the settings icon in the top header.

Is my synchronization data private and secure?

Yes. All communications between the dashboard, databases, and Google APIs are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We do not sell user data. OAuth credentials are encrypted and stored in compliance with industry security protocols.
